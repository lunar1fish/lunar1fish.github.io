About Me!

Q: Who are you?

A: I'm a high schooler who likes playing instruments! I play clarinet and tenor saxophone, and I'm also an aspiring drum major. (fingers crossed!)

Q: How long have you been playing?

A: I've been playing saxophone in general for over 5 years now; crazy how time flies like that! And I've been playing clarinet for about 3 years now, yet somehow it's my better instrument. 

Q: What else would you want to play?

A: All the woodwinds! Maybe starting with Eb Clarinet, and slowly expanding my reach. One day I'll be strong enough to play flute! I'm also hoping to be able to pick up percussion as well.

Q: What else do you do?

A: Honestly just whatever I feel like, but a lot of video games; I've been doing a LOT of modded Minecraft recently, and it's been fun suffering through Modern Industrialization. 

Q: What's the point of this site?

A: Helping push myself to practice daily! It's definitely something I need to improve on and so this will hopefully help me practice more often.
